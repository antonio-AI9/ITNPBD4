This repository contains a Data Science project for **V.Ger Travel**, a fictional travel company managing hotels, resorts, car rentals, and charter flights. As the appointed Chief Data Officer (CDO), the goal is to introduce analytical methods that improve forecasting, customer insight, and operational efficiency.


## Cases

### **1. Hotel Booking Demand Forecasting (Main Case)**

* **Objective:** Predict monthly hotel bookings.
* **Data:** Synthetic time series.
* **Methods:** Decomposition, ARIMA/SARIMA.
* **Value:** Supports staffing, pricing, and resource planning.

### **2. Customer Churn Prediction - Briefly Described**

* **Objective:** Identify customers likely to stop using hotel/flight services.
* **Methods:** Classification models.
* **Value:** Enables targeted retention efforts.

### **3. Customer Satisfaction Analysis - Briefly Described**

* **Objective:** Understand drivers of satisfaction across hotels and resorts.
* **Methods:** Regression, feature importance.
* **Value:** Improves service quality and customer experience.
